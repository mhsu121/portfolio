HI, I'M MICHELLE!

There are two things that I’ve always had a passion for: creating things and solving problems. Those passions have taken different forms throughout the years, from drawing on the walls of my parents’ house to participating in high school Math Olympics to designing flyers and logos for various clubs in college. Most recently, I’ve been working on several personal design projects of my own and sometimes serving as an informal interior designer for my friends interested in home improvement. In my current role as a strategy consultant at EY-Parthenon, I’ve helped clients in various industries ranging from consumer products to tech to education solve business problems and create strategies for future growth. I’m searching for a career in design because I want to be able to blend my two passions together—I’d like to solve problems through creation (and maybe identify some new problems to solve along the way).
